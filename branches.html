Our Branches

Kano

Located in the heart of Kano, NTIC Kano offers a stimulating and supportive learning environment for boys and girls from primary to SSS level. With a focus on innovation and creativity, the school aims to provide students with the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in the 21st century. From its state-of-the-art facilities to its dedicated teaching staff, NTIC Kano is a great choice for parents seeking a top-quality education for their children.

Kaduna

NTIC Kaduna also offers an exceptional educational experience for boys and girls from primary to SSS level. With a focus on academic excellence and character development, the school strives to provide students with the skills and knowledge they need to achieve their goals and reach their full potential. Whether you are looking for a rigorous academic program or a supportive learning environment, NTIC Kaduna has something to offer.

Abuja

Located in the heart of Abuja, NTIC Abuja offers a wide range of academic programs for boys and girls, from primary to SSS level. The school is renowned for its excellent academic record and its commitment to character development, with a strong focus on preparing students for success both in their academic pursuits and in their future careers.

Ogun

NTIC Ogun is a vibrant and dynamic school that offers an outstanding educational experience for boys and girls from JSS to SSS level. With a strong focus on innovation and creativity, the school aims to inspire students to explore their interests and develop their talents. From its state-of-the-art facilities to its experienced teaching staff, NTIC Ogun provides students with everything they need to succeed.

Yobe

With a focus on academic excellence and character development, the school aims to prepare students for success both in their academic pursuits and in their future careers. From its dedicated teaching staff to its innovative programs, NTIC Yobe is a great choice for parents seeking a top-quality education for their children.
