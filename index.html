Welcome to NTIC

Welcome to Nigerian Tulip International Colleges (NTIC), a group of world-class international schools located in Nigeria. Our schools offer a unique blend of Turkish and Nigerian curricula, providing our students with a comprehensive education that prepares them for success in a rapidly changing world. At NTIC, we are committed to fostering academic excellence, character development, and a passion for lifelong learning in all of our students. With state-of-the-art facilities, experienced teachers, and a vibrant community of learners from diverse backgrounds, NTIC offers a truly exceptional educational experience that sets our students on the path to achieving their full potential.

History

NTIC was founded in 1998 by a group of Nigerian and Turkish educators who shared a common vision of providing high-quality education to students in Nigeria. With the goal of promoting academic excellence, character development, and innovation, the founders set out to create a school that would meet the needs of a rapidly changing world. Over the years, NTIC has grown to become one of the leading schools in Nigeria, with campuses in Abuja, Kano, Kaduna, Ogun, and Yobe. Today, NTIC is recognized for its commitment to academic excellence, its innovative approach to education, and its focus on character development. With a diverse student body and a dedicated team of educators, NTIC continues to prepare students for success in the 21st century and beyond.

About NTIC

At NTIC, we believe that education is not just about acquiring knowledge, but about developing the skills and qualities necessary to become successful, responsible citizens of the world. That's why our curriculum is designed to provide a well-rounded education that includes not only academic subjects, but also extracurricular activities, community service, and character education. Our students are taught by highly qualified and experienced teachers who are committed to helping each student reach their full potential.

We use the latest teaching methods and technologies to ensure that our students are well-prepared for the challenges of the 21st century. NTIC has a strong emphasis on innovation and entrepreneurship, and we encourage our students to develop their creativity and critical thinking skills through projects and competitions.

We also have partnerships with leading universities and organizations around the world, providing our students with opportunities to participate in international programs and exchanges. NTIC is committed to providing a safe and supportive learning environment for all students. We have a zero-tolerance policy for bullying and discrimination, and our teachers and staff work closely with students and parents to ensure that everyone feels welcome and included.

We invite you to explore our website to learn more about NTIC and the many opportunities we offer for students to grow, learn, and succeed.
